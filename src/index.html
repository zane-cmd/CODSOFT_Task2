Black Pepper Biltong

Experience the bold, robust flavor of our Black Pepper Biltong. Crafted with premium cuts of beef and generously seasoned with freshly cracked black pepper, this biltong offers a perfect balance of savory and spicy. Each bite delivers a satisfying crunch and a burst of peppery goodness.

Geelvet Biltong

Delight in the rich, buttery taste of our Geelvet Biltong. Made from the finest cuts of beef with a luscious layer of yellow fat, this biltong offers an exceptional depth of flavor and a melt-in-your-mouth texture. Perfect for those who crave a premium, indulgent snack that showcases the best of traditional biltong craftsmanship

Boerewors Biltong

Indulge in the unique and hearty flavor of our Boerewors Biltong. Inspired by the classic South African sausage, this biltong is infused with a blend of coriander, cloves, and nutmeg. Each piece is a delightful fusion of traditional spices and premium beef, offering a truly authentic taste experience.
